Phase one: the Foundation

The intensive

The road to ethics and DevNet, 2026

The second half of 2026 runs from the summer push toward an early ethics submission (end of August, ahead of the 7 September Faculty deadline) through the autumn teaching, the taught workshops, and DevNet in December. Four strands run at once: the literature review that feeds everything, stepping up training, building relationships with partners in Jamaica, Tonga, and Comoros, now opened, and drafting the research instruments.
