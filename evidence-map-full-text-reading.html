How this map was made. During full text screening and adjudication, every study received a written reading note recording what it is, what it found, and what it offers the FAR-SIDS design (variables, methods, warnings, and context). The themes above were drawn out of those notes rather than from keywords: a study appears under a theme only where its note carries a concrete contribution to it. Counts will move as adjudication closes; the map is dated July 2026 and will be redrawn at data extraction. The title and abstract stage has its own map, linked below.
